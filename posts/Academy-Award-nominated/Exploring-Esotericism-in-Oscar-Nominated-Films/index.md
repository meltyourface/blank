The Academy Award nominated films often reflect themes that delve into the magickal and occult realms. These themes offer insights and unveil hidden truths about the human condition and the nature of reality.

### 2.2 The Hero's Journey: The archetypal journey of the hero has long been a part of the magickal and esoteric tradition. Nominated films that have exemplified this theme, such as The Lord of the Rings series (2001-2003), offer a profound exploration of the human psyche and its potential for growth and transformation.

The Academy Awards nominees frequently showcase practices rooted in the magickal arts, offering a glimpse into these ancient and often misunderstood traditions.
