  Craft a dark and mesmerizing sonnet paying tribute to Agarica, the ancient Toad-witch of the Marshlands, highlighting her sorcerous powers, her loyal toxic toad familiars, her mastery over ethereal illusions, and the way her capricious influence has shaped the fate of the Marshlands and its inhabitants throughout the ages. In marshlands old, where whispers slink and glide,
By twisting vines, Agarica's domain
Reveals the shadows lurking deep inside,
Her craft of mystic sorcery and bane.

Her toxic toads, with eyes of murky glow,
Attend their queen in homage and in dread,
Their virulent defense casts on her foe
With sacred croak, the bane of others' stead.

Ethereal illusions manifest,
The veils that shroud the hallowed marshlands' core,
She casts upon the hearts that try to wrest,
Their mooring lost in shadows evermore.

Capricious fate, the mistress of these lands,
Doth dance with Agarica's ancient hands.
