**Explanation**: The primary objective of reappropriating goddesses from diverse cultures within the context of the Reappropriated Goddess movement and modern esoteric practices is to regain women's empowerment and influence within the spiritual sphere by restoring lost ancient knowledge.

Throughout history, many ancient civilizations believed in goddesses and revered them as powerful symbols of creation, wisdom, and fertility. However, as patriarchy took root and became the dominant social and political structure in many societies, goddess worship gradually diminished, leading to the suppression and loss of much of the wisdom and knowledge associated with these feminine deities.

In recent years, there has been a resurgence of interest in goddesses, particularly within the context of alternative spiritual practices, feminism, and esotericism. The Reappropriated Goddess movement seeks to reclaim and restore the lost ancient knowledge surrounding goddesses and feminine power by studying historical texts, engaging in experiential practices, and incorporating feminine symbols and deities into modern spiritual practices.

By studying and reinterpreting the role of goddesses from different cultures, followers of the Reappropriated Goddess movement aim to balance the gendered representation of the divine, restore women's spiritual empowerment, and reconnect with a more inclusive, egalitarian spiritual understanding.

It is essential to note that the Reappropriated Goddess movement does not solely promote the worship of female deities and is not exclusively focused on establishing a new religious order centered on goddess worship. Its main goal is to empower women and rediscover the wisdom, knowledge, and spiritual practices associated with goddesses in various cultures.

In conclusion, the primary objective of the Reappropriated Goddess movement in modern esoteric practices is to restore lost ancient knowledge surrounding goddesses from diverse cultures, thereby regaining women's empowerment and influence within the spiritual sphere. The movement is characterized by a deep respect for cultural context, historical accuracy, and symbolism, aiming for a more balanced, inclusive spiritual understanding, and practice.
