For instance, the ancient Babylonians, Egyptians, and Romans consulted astrologers to plan important events, such as battles and political decisions, to ensure favorable outcomes. In this context, assassination plotters who sought to maximize their chance of success might have been inclined to choose a date and time with astrologically favorable conditions.
