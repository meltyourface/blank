The XI°, on the other hand, represents the end product of the IX°, culminating in an adept's mastery of these energies and their ultimate unity with the divine. In this context, the degrees symbolize different stages on the path to spiritual transformation, with the XI° serving as the culmination and fruition of successful iterations of the IX° magick.
