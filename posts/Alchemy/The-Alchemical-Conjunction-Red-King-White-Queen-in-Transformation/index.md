He exemplifies aspects of fire, intuition, and passion. Conversely, the White Queen embodies the feminine essence, holding connections with the element of mercury, the spirit, the moon, and the enigmatic energy of the 'albedo' or 'whiteness'. She encompasses the opposing attributes of water, receptivity, and rationality. 

Together, the Red King and White Queen epitomize the dualistic nature of existence, with their marriage symbolizing the union of opposites, the blending of the soul and spirit, and the attainment of harmony and perfection.
