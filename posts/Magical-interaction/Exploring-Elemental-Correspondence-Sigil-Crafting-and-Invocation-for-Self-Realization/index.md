Sigil crafting, on the other hand, is a more focused aspect of magical interaction. By designing and concentrating on a specific sigil, a magical practitioner is able to harness the power and energy associated with the symbol, thereby manifesting their intention into the physical or spiritual realms.

The art of invocation, a vital aspect of magical practice, involves summoning a specific entity or energy into one's presence through the use of ritual, prayer, or other means.
