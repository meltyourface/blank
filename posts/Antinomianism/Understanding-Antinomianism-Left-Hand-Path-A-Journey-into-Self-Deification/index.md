Antinomianism is a religious doctrine rooted in the belief that one can achieve salvation or spiritual liberation by transcending established moral and social norms. Unlike the more traditional Right-Hand Path that often embraces religious dogma, community values, and normative moral codes, the Left-Hand Path seeks a path of individualism, self-reliance, and personal empowerment that may entail the exploration of taboo or morally ambivalent territories.
