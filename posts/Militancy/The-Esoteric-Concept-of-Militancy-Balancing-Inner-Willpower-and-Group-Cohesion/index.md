In order to fully explain the concept of Militancy within the sphere of occultism and esotericism, the answer must consider three intertwined aspects of this phenomenon: the inner realm, the outer realm, and the bridge between the two realms through initiation and magickal practices.

The first aspect concerns the inner realm, where an individual's willpower is honed. This is crucial to the development of one's magical capabilities and self-transformation. In this context, Militancy refers to the strengthening and fortification of one's inner self through discipline, concentration, and regular practice. In esoteric terms, "as above, so below," suggests that if an individual's willpower (the microcosm), is strong, it can be reflected in the macrocosm (the outer realm).

The second aspect pertains to the outer realm, where the concept of Militancy encompasses the understanding of occult warfare and the promotion of group cohesion among practitioners. Occult warfare involves both psychic and magical battles fought on unseen planes, as well as utilizing magick and other esoteric techniques to influence events and individuals in the physical world. Group cohesion, on the other hand, fosters willpower, focus, and strength among practitioners, as they work towards common goals driven by similar beliefs and objectives.

Now, having established these aspects, we will explore how the two can be brought together simultaneously through initiation and the application of esoteric techniques.

Initiation, a cornerstone of the esoteric path, is fundamental in enabling an individual to both navigate and synthesize the inner and outer realms of Militancy. The initiation process, often characterized by immersive experiences and symbolic rituals, allows the initiate to gain insights and awaken inner latent abilities. These abilities enable one to align with higher energies, transcend the mundane, and connect to a higher source of wisdom. As a new initiate embraces these energies, they often experience a rapid expansion of willpower, discipline, and focus.

Magical symbols and rituals are instrumental in realizing the simultaneous manifestations of Militancy in both personal and group-based practices. Symbols have the power to directly invoke energies and entities associated with them, serving as focal points and bridges for the practitioner's will. In a group setting, the use of shared symbols and rituals can further magnify the effect by creating a collective resonance amongst practitioners.

One such example is the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP), a preparatory ritual that clears the practitioner's space and reinforces their willpower. By performing the LBRP or similar rituals consistently, the practitioner disciplines their mind, develops focus, and strengthens their energetic presence, which positively impacts their lives in both the inner and outer realms.

In summary, the concept of Militancy in the context of occultism and esotericism spans both inner willpower and outer cohesion and warfare, and these manifestations can be realized simultaneously through initiation and the application of magickal symbols and rituals. The acquisition, development, and application of knowledge and techniques undertaken by initiates and practitioners enhance their own personal magical prowess and strengthen the collective will and effectiveness of the esoteric community as a whole.
