In order to construct a multifaceted and ethically sound magic spell that embodies the principles of correspondence, mental transmutation, and synchronicity, and combines the elements of earth, air, fire, and water, we must first understand each of these principles and elements. Then, we can consider the necessary precautions, appropriate timing, and unique attributes of each element in creating the comprehensive ritual for spiritual transformation.

The principles of correspondence and mental transmutation are derived from the Hermetic principles, which aid in our understanding of universal truths. The principle of correspondence illustrates the connection between the macrocosm (the universe) and the microcosm (the individual), stating that "as above, so below; as within, so without." The principle of mental transmutation refers to the ability to change one's mental state, thoughts, or emotions, thus influencing the external reality. Synchronicity, a concept introduced by Carl Jung, refers to the idea of seemingly unrelated events happening simultaneously, conveying a meaningful connection between them.

Now let's examine the four elements—earth, air, fire, and water—and the significance of each in our comprehensive ritual:

1. Earth: The element of earth represents physicality, grounding, stability, and manifestation. In our spiritual transformation ritual, the earth element serves as the foundation for bringing our intentions into reality.

2. Air: The element of air represents intellect, communication, and clarity of thought. In this context, air purifies our intentions and thoughts, allowing us to convey them effectively.

3. Fire: The element of fire signifies action, transformation, and purification. It carries the energies of creativity, passion, and determination, helping us to overcome obstacles and invoke change.

4. Water: The element of water embodies emotions, intuition, and spiritual healing. It aids us in fostering emotional balance and connecting with our inner selves to decipher the spiritual messages required for our transformation.

With this understanding, we can construct the comprehensive ritual for spiritual transformation, considering the necessary precautions, proper timing, and unique qualities of each element:

1. Precautions: Before attempting any magic spell, it is crucial to be in a calm, grounded, and focused mental state to ensure that our intentions are pure and align with our ethical values. Maintaining a positive and respectful attitude towards the practices and energies involved is essential for the ritual's success.

2. Timing: The best time to perform the ritual is during the waxing moon phase when the energies are conducive to growth and manifesting intentions. Choose a date that resonates with your personal numerology or astrological transits for added effectiveness.

3. Ritual Steps:

   a. Begin by cleansing and consecrating the ritual space using incense (air element) and smudging with sage (fire element).
   
   b. Create a circle using salt (earth element) to represent grounding and stability, and place a chalice containing water (water element) in the center to symbolize emotional connection and spiritual healing.
   
   c. Invoke the four elements in their respective directions (earth in the North, air in the East, fire in the South, and water in the West) to harmonize and balance their energies within the ritual space.
   
   d. Meditate on your intention for spiritual transformation, focusing on transmuting any mental or emotional blocks and connecting to the universe (correspondence principle) for guidance and wisdom.
   
   e. Perform an act of release, such as burning a written intention in a fireproof dish (fire element), to signify the transformation of your inner being.
   
   f. Close the ritual by thanking the elements and releasing their energies, dismantling the circle, and grounding yourself by touching the earth.
   
In summary, the ethically sound magic spell for spiritual transformation involves understanding the principles of correspondence, mental transmutation, and synchronicity, as well as the unique qualities of the elements earth, air, fire, and water. By taking necessary precautions, choosing proper timing, and harmonizing the elements in a comprehensive ritual, you can manifest an intended spiritual transformation, aligning your inner self with the universal energies to forge a path of growth and enlightenment.
