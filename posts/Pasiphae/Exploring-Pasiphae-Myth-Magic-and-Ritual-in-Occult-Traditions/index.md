### Chapter 1: The Mythological Background of Pasiphae

Pasiphae, a figure shrouded in mystique, originates from Greek mythology. Daughter of the sun god Helios and the Oceanid nymph Perse, she is also the mother of the legendary Minotaur and wife of King Minos of Crete. With her divine ancestry, Pasiphae possesses exceptional knowledge of the magical arts and of all secret things. In this section, we shall delve into the mythical narrative surrounding this enigmatic figure and provide a foundation to appreciate her role in occult traditions.
