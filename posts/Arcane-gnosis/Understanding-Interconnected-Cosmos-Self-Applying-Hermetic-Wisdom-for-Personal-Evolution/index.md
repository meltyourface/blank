Answering this knowledge challenge requires us to first understand the foundational concepts and principles of the ancient hermetic axiom "As above, so below" and the relationship between the macrocosm and microcosm. Following this foundation, we will then explore how arcane gnosis connects to and reflects this cosmic law, as well as the practical implications for seekers of wisdom.

Foundational Concepts
"As above, so below" is one of the core principles of Hermeticism, which is an esoteric tradition rooted in the teachings of Hermes Trismegistus, credited with having authored the Hermetic Corpus, a series of sacred texts. This axiom suggests that the same fundamental patterns and principles govern all levels of reality, from the largest (cosmic or macrocosm) to the smallest (individual or microcosm). Essentially, it points to the belief that everything in our observed reality is interconnected; what occurs in the heavens has a direct impact on and relationship to what happens on Earth.

Macrocosm and Microcosm
Macrocosm is a term used to describe the larger universal scheme of things, including celestial bodies, the cosmos, and the transcendent. Microcosm, however, refers to the smaller, more finite elements within the universe, such as human beings, plants, animals, and even cells. The interconnectivity between the macrocosm and microcosm is based on the principle that what occurs on one level is reflected on other levels. 

Arcane Gnosis and Cosmic Law
Arcane gnosis, which refers to the deep, profound, and often hidden knowledge within the realm of the esoteric, occult, and spiritual, is intrinsically connected to the cosmic law "As above, so below." The principles of arcane gnosis often involve the exploration and understanding of both the divine and human realms, their inherently interconnected nature, and the ways in which they influence one another.

For example, in traditional alchemy, the process of transmutation - an allegory for the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment - involves refining and transforming base metals into noble metals (such as gold), which embodies the macrocosm/microcosm relationship. By studying the workings of alchemy on a material level, these alchemists sought to reveal and understand the spiritual truths at work within the broader cosmos and the self - thus demonstrating the interconnectedness between the microcosm (the alchemical process) and the macrocosm (the spiritual truth).

Applying This Understanding to Individual Evolution
For a seeker of wisdom looking to apply the understanding of "As above, so below," the journey begins with acknowledging and appreciating the profound connections between the self and the universe. As the macrocosm and microcosm are interconnected, learning from the natural, social, and spiritual realms in the world outside of oneself can be incredibly insightful for those seeking realization and enlightenment.

Moreover, self-reflection, introspection, and inner work can help a seeker draw on the knowledge they've gained and mirror the principles of arcane gnosis to instigate transformation within themselves. Practices such as meditation, dream analysis, and creative expression can help cultivate the self-awareness necessary to apply cosmic law to individual growth.

Finally, recognizing that the self and the wider universe have a symbiotic relationship is essential to enact meaningful change. By understanding the sacred nature of "As above, so below," a seeker can connect their life's path to the broader context of the universe, helping to align their personal journey with broader cosmic patterns and energies.

In summary, the ancient Hermetic axiom "As above, so below" underpins the belief that the macrocosm and microcosm are profoundly interconnected. Arcane gnosis encapsulates this cosmic law, illustrating the intricate relationships that govern all levels of reality. To embody this understanding in individual evolution, seekers of wisdom should explore both the outer world and their inner selves, applying the insights gleaned from these investigations to their own personal growth and embracing interconnectedness with the wider universe.
