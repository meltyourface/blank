Mercury, on the other hand, symbolizes the spirit or the divine essence that links the material and the spiritual realms, allowing for communication and connection between them.

1. Nigredo (Blackening): This stage involves confrontation with one's shadow self and the dissolution of the ego, represented by Salt's connection to the material plane.
2. Albedo (Whitening): In this stage, the alchemist refines and purifies their being through deep introspection and self-discovery. Sulfur's transformative force plays a crucial role in this process, activating the alchemist's inner albedo.
3. Rubedo (Reddening): This final stage represents the synthesis and perfection of the alchemist's spiritual and material aspects, attained through a union with the divine. Mercury facilitates this union, as it forms a bridge between the material and spiritual realms.
