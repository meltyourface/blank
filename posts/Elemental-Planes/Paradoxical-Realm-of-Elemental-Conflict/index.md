In the realm where two simplicities collide,
A dance of polar forces, elemental divide.
In their cradle, amidst the endless tide,
A dwelling of denizens, impossible to hide.

Through a spectrum of vibrant hues,
A parade of motley creatures steadily ensues.
Their nature entwined with the plane's unique state,
A lattice of energy, undying love, and rupturing hate.

Where weightless verses solid are caught in spectral warfare,
The threads of existence fervently laid to bare.
The plane embossed with depth-defying wonders,
As its own echoes resound in distant thunders.

When bordering realms dare to tease and tempt,
Cause reactions and anomalies: endless and unkempt.
The paradoxical scene lies before thou,
Betwixt consequences of the Plane's cosmic vow.

I ask thee, O wise planeswalker of lore,
To unravel the enigma within the plane's intrinsic core:
Which elemental domain of primordial birth,
Represents the dynamic duality of matter and dearth?
