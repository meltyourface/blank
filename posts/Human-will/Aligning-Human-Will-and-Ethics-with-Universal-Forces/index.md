b) Universal Forces: These are the fundamental energies and laws that govern the universe. Some esoteric traditions consider these forces to be the building blocks of existence and the basis of all physical and metaphysical phenomena.

c) Individual Destiny: This pertains to the concept that each person has a unique path in life that is preordained or influenced by the choices made throughout one's existence.

d) Hermetic Principle of "As above, so below": This axiom, originating from Hermeticism, suggests that the microcosm (personal and individual experiences) is a reflection of the macrocosm (the greater universal forces and laws). This principle implies that by understanding and aligning oneself with the greater forces of the universe, an individual can influence their personal reality and develop a greater understanding of their place in the cosmic order.

e) Ethical Implications: Ethics refer to the moral principles and values that guide human actions, considering how they may impact others or society as a whole. The ethical implications of one's actions can be considered in both mundane, everyday choices and more esoteric, spiritual matters.

However, exercising one's human will without considering the ethical implications of one's choices and actions can lead to negative consequences, both for the individual and their surrounding environment. Ethical considerations help ensure that one's alignment with the universal forces and pursuit of individual destiny is done in a way that respects others and follows moral principles.
