The Order provided graded advancement through initiation rituals and a series of "Knowledge Lectures" that offered deep study of the esoteric arts. Central to the Golden Dawn's philosophy is the concept of "as above, so below," which denotes the interconnectivity between the microcosm and the macrocosm as a fundamental principle of the universe.
