The industrial age has led to unprecedented levels of resource exploitation, resulting in pollution, deforestation, climate change, and the loss of biodiversity. These changes have disrupted the delicate balance of our ecosystems.

Economic Consequences:

However, this has also led to vast disparities in wealth distribution, exploitation of natural and human resources, and an often unsustainable consumer-driven society.

In accordance with the Hermetic principle, we can observe a correlation between our economic systems and their cosmic implications.
