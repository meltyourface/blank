The silver cord is said to have a dual function, acting as an anchor to the physical body and as a conduit for transferring energy, information, and sensations between the physical and astral planes.

The silver cord connects these distinct aspects within the multi-dimensional nature of the individual, ensuring their safe and harmonious interaction with the various layers of existence.

In relation to astral travel, the silver cord serves as a lifeline that helps guide and secure the astral body in its journeys across the planes of existence. A metaphorical example of the silver cord's function can be seen in the idea of a boat tethered to a dock - where the boat (the astral body) can float and drift away, while not losing connection to the land (the physical body). It's this tethering function that enables the astral traveler to retain an awareness of their physical body while exploring other dimensions, ensuring their eventual return to the dense material plane.

This apparent contradiction might reflect different cultural beliefs and understandings about the afterlife and astral travel, leading to varying interpretations of the silver cord's nature and capabilities.
