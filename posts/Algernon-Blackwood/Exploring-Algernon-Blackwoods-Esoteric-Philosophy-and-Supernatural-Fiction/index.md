Introduction: Algernon Blackwood's Impact on the World of the Occult

Algernon Blackwood, born in 1869, was an English author and prominent figure in the world of the occult. His writing spanned across multiple genres including horror, fantasy, and supernatural fiction, garnering a loyal following who often engaged with the esoteric themes found in his works. Blackwood's impact on the occult cannot be understated, as he was not only an avid practitioner but also an influential spokesperson for the community. Blackwood's most significant works include his short stories and novellas.
