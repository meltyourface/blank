Answering this knowledge challenge requires a deep dive into the concepts of occultism, esotericism, transformation through shadow work, and the dualities of existence. We will explore how balancing light and darkness can contribute to a personal affirmation of life and the benefits it can have both for the individual and for collective humanity.

1. Occultism and Esotericism: 
Occultism and esotericism involve the study and practice of hidden knowledge and spiritual truths beyond mainstream understanding. In essence, they emphasize the exploration of inner, subjective experiences as a means to attain personal growth and empowerment.

2. Shadow Self and Transformational Power:
The concept of the "shadow self" comes from the work of Swiss psychiatrist Carl Jung, who theorized that individuals possess both conscious and unconscious aspects of their psyche. The shadow self represents the repressed, undesirable, and often dark aspects of our personalities that we may not be aware of. By engaging with and confronting our shadow selves, we can acknowledge and integrate these darker aspects, ultimately leading to self-acceptance, growth, and personal transformation.

3. The Paradox of Wholeness through Embracing Dualities:
Embracing the dualities of existence means acknowledging and accepting the opposing forces within ourselves and the world around us, such as light and darkness, good and evil, and life and death. Paradoxically, it is through this acceptance that we can attain a sense of unity and wholeness both internally and externally.

4. Constructing a Personal Affirmation of Life:
To create a personal affirmation of life that integrates both light and darkness, one must first recognize their shadow self and the dualities present in their own being. This can be achieved through self-exploration, meditation, journaling, and other introspective practices. Once a person gains an awareness of their dualities, they can develop a mindset that embraces and harmonizes these aspects.

A sample affirmation might be: "I acknowledge and embrace the light and darkness within me, and I honor the dualities of existence. Through the integration of my whole self, I experience personal growth and understand my role in the interconnected web of humanity."

5. The Ultimate Benefits for the Individual and Collective Human Experience:
By engaging in shadow work and embracing the dualities of existence, individuals can achieve a heightened level of self-awareness, personal growth, and inner harmony. As more individuals commit to this process, a collective transformation can occur, fostering increased empathy, compassion, and understanding in our interactions with others. This shift can contribute to the betterment of interpersonal relationships, communities, and society as a whole by promoting an increased recognition of our shared humanity.

In conclusion, engaging with one's shadow self and embracing the dualities of existence carry significant transformational power that can foster personal growth, self-expression, and deep understanding. Through the construction of a personal affirmation of life that authentically integrates both light and darkness, an individual can catalyze not only their own growth, but also contribute to the greater collective human experience. This approach encourages the pursuit of wholeness, interconnectedness, and acceptance of our complex and multifaceted nature.
