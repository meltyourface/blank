In shadows' birth and slumbers' gaze,
A riddle's heart enthralls and strays:
Lu'iyat al-kamr, the dark moon's gleam,
Lilith's song, neath whispers' seam.

- - -

Ur-šīr šilum mtu-lilim,
Abrak-hathāla mī'is šanē.
Hašṭāma tūbhê wa'šrūqē,
Ištu'n-nākhṛu bah elishā.

- - -

Coils of time, seductive hold,
In malediction's grasp enfold.
The serpent's shun, the screech owl's cry,
Awake and stir, O Lilith, rise!

- - -

Mašīla kata, šupe'b'qudsi!
Be-monî araf il draxū-tam.
Gargatiš-da'yar kšāf-tulā
Nērgal di'b'lilin rezāyā.
