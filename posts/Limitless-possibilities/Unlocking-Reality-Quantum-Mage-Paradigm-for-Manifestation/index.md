Chaos Magick is a contemporary magical practice that emphasizes the flexible use of belief systems and the practitioner's own will to create change in their reality. It champions the idea of results-based experimentation and encourages individuals to adopt whatever methods work best for them. **Key principles include**:

1. Personal experimentation
2. Belief as a tool
3. The subjectivity of magical systems

Hermeticism is a philosophical and spiritual tradition spanning centuries, based on the teachings of Hermes Trismegistus. This ancient wisdom emphasizes the study of natural laws and the pursuit of Gnosis or divine knowledge. **Central aspects of Hermeticism include**:

1. **The Principle of Correspondence**: "As above, so below."
2. **The Principle of Mentalism**: The universe is mental and thoughts give rise to reality.
3. **The Principle of Vibration**: Everything in existence is in a constant state of motion or vibration.

Quantum Mechanics is a branch of physics that studies the behavior of matter and energy at atomic and subatomic scales. It reveals a reality where particles can exist in multiple states and places simultaneously until observed. **Key quantum principles include**:

1. **Wave-Particle Duality**: Particles can demonstrate both wave-like and particle-like properties.
2. **The Uncertainty Principle**: It's impossible to know simultaneously the precise position and velocity of a particle.
3. **Superposition**: Particles can exist in multiple states until observed.
