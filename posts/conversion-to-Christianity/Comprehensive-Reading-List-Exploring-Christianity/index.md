## I. Foundational Scriptures

1. The Holy Bible (recommended translations: New International Version, English Standard Version, New American Standard Bible)
   - Old Testament (Key Books: Genesis, Exodus, Psalms, Isaiah, Proverbs)
   - New Testament (Key Books: Matthew, Mark, Luke, John, Acts, Romans, 1 Corinthians, Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians)

### II. Church History and Historical Context

1. "The Story of Christianity" by Justo L. González
2. "Church History in Plain Language" by Bruce Shelley
3. "The Early Church Fathers" edited by Philip Schaff
4. "Eusebius' Ecclesiastical History" by Eusebius Pamphili

### III. Systematic and Biblical Theology

1. "Systematic Theology: An Introduction to Biblical Doctrine" by Wayne Grudem
2. "Christian Theology" by Millard Erickson
3. "The Institutes of the Christian Religion" by John Calvin
4. "Theology for the Community of God" by Stanley Grenz

### IV. Apologetics and Reasoning

1. "Mere Christianity" by C.S. Lewis
2. "The Case for Christ" by Lee Strobel
3. "The Reason for God: Belief in an Age of Skepticism" by Timothy Keller
4. "Apologetics to the Glory of God: An Introduction" by John Frame

### V. Biographies and Personal Narratives

1. "The Confessions" by St. Augustine
2. "The Seven Storey Mountain" by Thomas Merton
3. "Surprised by Joy" by C.S. Lewis
4. "The Pilgrim's Progress" by John Bunyan

### VI. Devotional and Spiritual Growth

1. "The Imitation of Christ" by Thomas à Kempis
2. "The Practice of the Presence of God" by Brother Lawrence
3. "My Utmost for His Highest" by Oswald Chambers
4. "Celebration of Discipline: The Path to Spiritual Growth" by Richard Foster

### VII. Christian Ethics and Moral Theology

1. "The Cost of Discipleship" by Dietrich Bonhoeffer
2. "Ethics" by Stanley Hauerwas
3. "The Moral Vision of the New Testament" by Richard Hays
4. "Kingdom Ethics: Following Jesus in Contemporary Context" by Glen Stassen and David Gushee

### VIII. Perspectives on Christian Life and Practice

1. "Desiring God: Meditations of a Christian Hedonist" by John Piper
2. "Radical: Taking Back Your Faith from the American Dream" by David Platt
3. "The Divine Conspiracy: Rediscovering Our Hidden Life in God" by Dallas Willard
4. "The Pursuit of Holiness" by Jerry Bridges

This comprehensive reading list should provide a well-rounded exploration of Christianity for seekers, covering fundamental doctrines, historical context, theological perspectives, and engaging personal narratives. It is vital for the seeker to approach this list prayerfully and with an open heart, allowing God to speak and guide them in their spiritual journey.
