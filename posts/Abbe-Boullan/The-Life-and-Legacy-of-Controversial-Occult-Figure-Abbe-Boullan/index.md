He believed that as a spiritual leader, he was anointed as the representative of this divine feminine power. Boullan's teachings were an amalgamation of Christian Gnosticism, Kabbalistic elements, and his own original ideas. His theology included concepts such as the redemption of humanity through the union of the divine feminine and one's inner nature, the notion of spiritual warfare, and the importance of sexual energy.

### VII. Conclusion

Abbe Boullan's life and work serve as a testament to the daring and controversial nature of the esoteric world during the 19th century.
