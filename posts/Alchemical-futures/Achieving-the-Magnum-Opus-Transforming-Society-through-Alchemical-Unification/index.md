The Magnum Opus, or the "Great Work," represents the pinnacle of Alchemical pursuit. Achieving it implies the complete unification of spiritual, philosophical, and physical realms in an alchemical future. Within this newly awakened paradigm, humanity would be profoundly transformed on multiple levels. 

We must first explore the consequences of mastering inner transmutation, which refers to the alchemist's pursuit of perfecting their soul and attaining spiritual enlightenment. This transformative process mirrors the transmutation of base metals like lead into gold, symbolizing the profound impact on human consciousness. In this scenario, each person would achieve a state of self-actualization and inner balance, cultivating virtue, wisdom, and personal power. Our society would experience a remarkable upswing in emotional intelligence, empathy, and insight into the interconnected nature of reality.

As humanity perfects their inner selves, societal progress would naturally follow. Concepts such as prejudice, hatred, and crime would decline significantly since individuals would understand and appreciate the interconnected nature of all beings. Poverty, warfare, and social unrest could reasonably be expected to diminish as well, as newfound collective wisdom drives more compassionate and united decision-making.

Another consequence would be the advancement of knowledge and understanding in the fields of science, technology, and the arts. Humanity could unlock hidden potentialities within nature, leading to unparalleled discoveries in medicine, energy production, and teleportation. The unification of the spiritual, philosophical, and physical realms would vastly expand our modes of thought and creativity, enabling the realization of a fully-functioning utopia, providing unprecedented levels of well-being and fulfillment.

However, there would be potential ethical considerations to keep in mind. The power to transmute matter and transform societies could be susceptible to misuse by those seeking personal gain or power. Access to these unprecedented abilities might need to be carefully monitored and controlled to prevent harmful outcomes or the concentration of power in the hands of a few individuals. Additionally, philosophical and religious conflicts could arise as humanity grapples with the implications of their new status, necessitating a period of adaptation to the emerging reality.

In summary, the key themes of the Magnum Opus being achieved through the unification of the spiritual, philosophical, and physical realms include:

1. Mastery of inner transmutation leading to profound spiritual and emotional growth in individuals, fostering empathy, compassion, and unity.
2. Societal progress characterized by the decline in social unrest, warfare, poverty, prejudice, and crime, as humanity embraces its interconnected nature.
3. Potential advancements in science, technology, and the arts leading to breakthrough discoveries in medicine, energy production, and transportation.
4. The need to address ethical considerations, such as preventing the misuse of alchemical power, possible philosophical conflicts, and maintaining balance in the distribution of knowledge and abilities.

The consequences of the Magnum Opus's achievement hold immense potential for human evolution and realization of a utopic future. However, careful attention must be paid to ethical concerns and adaptability, ensuring society navigates this new reality with wisdom and foresight.
