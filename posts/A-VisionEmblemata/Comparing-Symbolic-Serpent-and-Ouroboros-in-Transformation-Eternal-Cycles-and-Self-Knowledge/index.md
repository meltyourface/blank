The Ouroboros, in Hermeticism, is a symbol depicting a serpent or dragon eating its tail, representing the cyclic nature of the universe, eternal return, and the concept of self-knowledge.

For the Serpent in A VisionEmblemata, this knowledge comes with the realization and embracing of one's inherent divinity. In Hermeticism, the Ouroboros serves as a reminder that self-knowledge and understanding are essential in realizing the eternal nature of reality.

2. ****Visual representation****: While the Serpent in A VisionEmblemata is typically depicted as a snake shedding its skin or intertwined with other objects, the Ouroboros in Hermeticism adopts a circular shape, explicitly highlighting the eternal cyclic nature in its presentation.
