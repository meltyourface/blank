Hold your breath for a count of three, and then exhale slowly through your mouth, releasing all the air from your lungs. Repeat this process three times, feeling your body growing more relaxed and focused with each breath.

As you continue to breathe deeply, visualize a silver cord attached to the base of your spine. This cord represents the connection between your astral body and your physical form. Take a moment to feel its presence, and acknowledge its significance as a lifeline that will keep you connected to the earthly plane.

Now, begin to recite the sacred mantra: "Heth-tur-vah-reem". As you repeat the mantra, feel the vibration of the words resonating throughout your entire being.

To aid in the release of your astral body, we will use a specific breathing technique called the Pranic Breath. Inhale deeply through your nose, and as you exhale, feel a wave of energy being released, starting from the base of your spine, moving up to the crown of your head. This energy simultaneously stimulates and relaxes your astral body, allowing it to separate from your physical form.

Visualize your astral body floating a few inches above your physical body, gently swaying and acclimating to its newfound freedom. Notice that the silver cord remains intact, securely linking you back to the earthly plane.

Practice this guided meditation regularly to continue honing your astral projection skills and to deepen your connection to the hidden realms and sacred teachings of the occult.
