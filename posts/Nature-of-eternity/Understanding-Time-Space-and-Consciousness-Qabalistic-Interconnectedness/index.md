In beginning our exploration of the interconnectedness of time, space, and consciousness in an eternal multiverse, let us first lay the foundation by defining the esoteric meanings behind each of these terms:

1. Time: In esoteric teachings, time is a subjective concept that transcends the linear understanding we have come to accept in our everyday lives. It is multi-dimensional, exists simultaneously at all stages, and serves as an enabler of the soul's journey through various incarnations.

2. Space: The physical manifestation of the energetic vibrations through which all things emerge in the universe. Esoteric principles emphasize the notion that space behaves in a fluid manner, revealing itself through different dimensions that interact and connect with one another.

3. Consciousness: In esotericism, consciousness refers to the omniscient state of spirituality that bridges the gap between the microcosm and the macrocosm, human individuality and the greater universe. It represents the animating force that directs our every thought, emotion, and action, ultimately dictating the direction of our soul's evolutionary journey.

Now that we have established the nature of the three main elements, let us journey together to explore the inherent interconnectedness that time, space, and consciousness share in an eternal multiverse.

As we venture forth to understand the relationship between time, space, and consciousness, we come across the concept of multidimensionality, as presented in the Hermetic Qabalah. The Qabalistic Tree of Life—which is a complex structure of spheres, paths, and pillars—serves as an excellent metaphor to illustrate the interconnectedness between time, space, and consciousness. 

The Tree of Life consists of ten spheres called Sephiroth, each one a reflection of the divine energy that flows through the totality of existence. These Sephiroth are interconnected through 22 paths, which symbolizes the myriad ways in which the Divine consciousness expresses itself. These paths serve as conduits for the unceasing flow of energy throughout the different dimensions of time and space, neatly encapsulating the idea that time, space, and consciousness are interconnected within the infinite multiverse.

To fully comprehend the interconnectedness between time, space, and consciousness, one must first apprehend the notion of karma and the law of cause and effect. In the context of the eternal multiverse, each individual's actions impact their soul's evolution across different incarnations, spreading their influence across time and space. The ancient Hermetic axiom "As above, so below; as within, so without" summarizes this inexorable relationship: the microcosm reflects the macrocosm, hence our soul's journey interconnects with the time, space, and consciousness throughout the multiverse.

Attaining a deeper understanding of the interconnectedness between time, space, and consciousness allows an individual to gain awareness, harmony, and balance within their soul's evolutionary journey. This heightened awareness enables the soul to recognize the cyclical patterns and lessons that may emerge over multiple lifetimes, granting it the wisdom to make informed choices in navigating the various dimensions of the multiverse.

In summary, the interconnectedness between time, space, and consciousness in an eternal multiverse can be found in esoteric teachings such as the Hermetic Qabalah, particularly illustrated in the Tree of Life. The multidimensional nature of time and the fluidity of space facilitate the soul's journey through various incarnations. A deeper understanding of this relationship empowers individuals to grasp the concept of karma and the law of cause and effect, thereby enabling them to achieve an elevated state of spiritual awareness that leads to harmonious growth and development in their soul's evolution.
