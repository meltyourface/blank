2. ****Hermetic Principle "As above, so below"****: This is a central tenet of Hermeticism, originating in the Emerald Tablet of Hermes Trismegistus. It conveys the idea that there is a correspondence between the microcosm (individual reality) and the macrocosm (universal reality). Essentially, what occurs in one plane of existence reflects or influences what occurs in the other, and this relationship between the two planes is reciprocal.

With these definitions in mind, we can now explore the connections between true will manifestation and the Hermetic principle "As above, so below" and how these connections hold implications when aligning one's individual will with the collective consciousness to reshape the fabric of perceived reality.

The process of realizing and manifesting one's true will requires an understanding of not just one's personal desires and intentions, but also the broader collective and universal context. The Hermetic principle "As above, so below" highlights the interconnectedness of individual reality (microcosm) with the broader universal reality (macrocosm), which in turn implies that in order to manifest one's true will, it is necessary to recognize and align with these greater forces at work.

This alignment with the larger reality in fact amplifies the individual's ability to manifest their true will. In harnessing the power of correspondence, as emphasized by the Hermetic principle, one's true will is not merely expressed in the individual's localized experience but also resonates with the universal fabric, thereby increasing the impact and transformative potential of these intentions.

Moreover, connecting one's true will to the greater reality allows for a deeper understanding of how an individual's actions, thoughts, and intentions affect and are affected by other energies and processes within the universe. This understanding then contributes to a more effective utilization of one's true will,as it leads to a more conscientious approach to manifesting realities that are meaningful and influential on a broader scale.

As for the implications of aligning one's individual will with the collective consciousness, such alignment could allow for a fundamental reshaping of perceived reality. By embracing the notion that we are all interconnected and that our individual true wills are ultimately part of a more extensive universal tapestry, we can begin to shift our actions and intentions in a manner that reflects this greater unity. Consequently, our individual true wills would no longer be solely focused on personal desires and goals, but instead would seek to contribute positively to the collective reality.

To summarize, there is a direct connection between true will manifestation and the ancient Hermetic principle "As above, so below." Aligning one's individual will with the collective consciousness can reshape perceived reality by highlighting the interconnectedness and correspondence of all things, leading to a more profound understanding of the implications of our actions and intentions on the wider universe. By integrating these concepts, individuals can harmonize their true wills with the collective consciousness, attaining a more potent ability to shape the fabric of reality and contribute to the grand cosmic narrative.
