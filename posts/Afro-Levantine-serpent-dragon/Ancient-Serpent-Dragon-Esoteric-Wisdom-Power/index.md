The Afro-Levantine serpent-dragon embodies several key aspects of occult symbolism. As an amalgamation of serpents and dragons, the entity is associated with wisdom, transformation, and the creative/destructive duality. Serpent symbolism is prevalent throughout ancient cultures as the bearer of knowledge and holder of secrets. Snakes shed their skin, representing rebirth and transformation. Set fought against the forces of darkness and his battles with Apep, the serpentine chaos monster, represented order vs. chaos, creation vs. destruction.
