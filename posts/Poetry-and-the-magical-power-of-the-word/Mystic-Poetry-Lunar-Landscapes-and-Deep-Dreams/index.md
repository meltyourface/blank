In veils of velvet vision, vast and vivid,
Whispered wisps of winnowing winds wander,
By the boundless breath, braced and bewitched,
Three threads of thought, three flames unfettered:
 
The sibilant seraph soared singing, ciphered,
In cloistered crypts, concentric circles shone,
Merge the mortal mind with mystic mirrors,
Two echoes entwined, eerie and entranced:

Ephemeral etchings in echoed ether,
Vestiges of vanished voices, vernal,
Where wordless will weds wistful wavering,
Awaken the arcane, ancient and ageless:

Through lunar landscapes, lucid and liquescent,
Flowing feral fantasies weaving fate,
Dance to the dirge of deep sequestered dreams,
Our incantation imbued, inviolate.
