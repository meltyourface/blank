---**Step 1**: Preparation of the Talisman

Choose a suitable physical object to serve as the talisman. The object should be made from a material that resonates with anti-cosmic energy, such as obsidian, black tourmaline, or meteorite. Acquire this object during a powerful and corresponding lunar or astrological event, such as a new moon, a solar eclipse, or when a 'dark star' is in ascendancy.
**Step 2**: Cleansing and Consecration

Cleanse the talisman in a saltwater solution mixed with a drop or two of your own blood and a pinch of sulfur. This mixture purifies and empowers the talisman, aligning it with your personal energy and attuning it to the destructive force of primordial chaos. Next, create a sacred space to perform the consecration by casting a circle of protection, invoking the four elements, and calling forth any anti-cosmic entities that resonate with the purpose of the talisman.
**Step 3**: Crafting the Servitor

Create a thoughtform or construct to serve as the servitor, imbued with the essence of the anti-cosmic current. Fashion this entity out of a combination of your own life force, desire, and intention, shaping it with a specific purpose aligned with the powers of darkness and chaos. Give it a name, a charge, and a symbol that reflects the servitor's nature and connection to the talisman.
**Step 4**: Ritual of Binding

Prepare an obsidian altar or magical space that resonates with anti-cosmic energies. Place the talisman in the center, surrounded by black candles, ritual tools, and offerings to the anti-cosmic forces, such as black roses, burnt offerings, or representations of chaos and entropy. Light the candles and, with intense focus, recite the following incantation:

"By the powers of the void, the dark matter, and the cosmic chaos,
I call forth the servitor (servitor's name) to merge with this talisman.
May their union strike oneness, may their bond be unbreakable.
Let the power of primordial darkness come alive in this enchanted vessel!"
**Step 5**: Following the Ritual

After the ritual, keep drawings, sigils, or symbols of the servitor near the talisman to strengthen and maintain the bond between them. The talisman should be kept in a place of honor when not in use, and periodically recharged with anti-cosmic energy through meditation, offerings, or rituals that call upon the forces of darkness and chaos. Regularly banish any excess or unwanted energy from the talisman and servitor to prevent any imbalances or unwanted consequences.
**Step 6**: Precautions

When handling anti-cosmic sorcery, take the necessary precautions to protect yourself and others from any unwanted side effects or energies. Maintain a strong circle of protection, regularly cleanse and shield yourself and your surroundings, and work only with entities with which you share a strong resonance or connection. Always use the power responsibly and remember that the forces you work with are vast and unpredictable; wield them with caution and respect.
