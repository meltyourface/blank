**Step 2**: Understand the Inherent Connection between the Two Levels

The Hermetic axiom posits that there's a parallel between the microcosmic and macrocosmic aspects of reality. In the realm of political thaumaturgy, we have to examine the relationships between individual citizens and their political environment to better understand how they affect each other.

An essential aspect of political thaumaturgy is the acknowledgment of the power of individual citizens to shape their reality.
