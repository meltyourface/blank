Upon dark night, the void did breathe and sigh,
As whispers ebbed from realms beyond our gaze;
Rare secrets cast upon this mortal tie,
An astral dance in cryptic, madd'ning praise.

In pentagrams and sacred sigils drawn,
These runes of old, by cunning hand appear;
Their echoes hum, a mystic chant of brawn,
Deliver me, the initiate, from fear.

From hidden paths do ancient masters rise,
Of Her'nes moon, and Hecate's dread embrace;
Through swirling vortex, darkness eternal lies,
In dormant power, beyond the veils of lace.

Grasp cosmos' truth, let shadows be thy guide,
Infernal knowledge wields, the arcane tide.
