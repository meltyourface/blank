2. **Overview of karmic imbalances**:
Karma is a metaphysical principle rooted in Eastern spirituality, which suggests that every action, thought, or intention generates an equal reaction or consequence in the future. Karmic imbalances occur when one's actions, decisions, or behavior disrupt the flow of positive and negative energies in the universe, leading to personal or collective consequences that may manifest immediately or in future incarnations.

3. **Overview of the Hermetic principles**:
The Hermetic principles are a set of seven foundational concepts in Hermeticism, an ancient philosophical and spiritual tradition rooted in Hellenistic Egypt. These principles are believed to represent universal laws governing the cosmos, nature, and the human experience. **They are**:

### I. **The Principle of Mentalism**: "The All is Mind; The Universe is Mental."
### II. **The Principle of Correspondence**: "As above, so below; as below, so above."
### III. **The Principle of Vibration**: "Nothing rests; everything moves; everything vibrates."
### IV. **The Principle of Polarity**: "Everything is dual; everything has poles; everything has its pair of opposites."
### V. **The Principle of Rhythm**: "Everything flows, out and in; everything has its tides."
### VI. **The Principle of Cause and Effect**: "Every cause has its effect; every effect has its cause."
### VII. **The Principle of Gender**: "Gender is in everything; everything has its masculine and feminine principles."
