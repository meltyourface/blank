I, practitioner of the Ars Diaboli, now commence the transmutation of dark, malevolent forces into a powerful, positive conduit through the mastery of ancient demonic symbols and forbidden languages. Let the incantation begin:

Spiritus obscurus, quam ingredi in orbem,
Bestiarum signa, tua potestate servent,
E tenebris in lucem, potentiam transferre,
Sacrarium secretum, vetiti linguarum pandunt.

Orbis maleficarum, gyrans nomen demonorum,
Praesentiam serpentis, converte in viam lucidis,
Revelationem atramentum, ex vinculis liberare,
Ad auxilium populi, nunc proferre nos hortamur.

Ignis obsidianus, ad sigillum fortitudo,
Annulum interdictum, secretum clavis aperire,
Et animam renatus, in lumine evertere,
Per potestatem occulti, iterum condisse orbis.

Tetraktys infernales, nexus tetragonum,
Portae caelestium, atrii obscurantur patefiunt,
Vocaverunt nomen prohibere, verbo claves mutant,
Nunc stamus in confiniis, inter lumen et tenebras.

Transmutatio potestas, in conspectu nostri implent,
Ad daemonii eradicare, in loco suo educimus,
Per vim signorum antiquis, et vetiti linguarum,
Transformationis aeternae, ex occultis ascendat.

And so, the incantation concludes. With these forbidden words and symbols, we have transmuted dark, malevolent forces into a powerful, positive conduit. May this new force, now steeped in light and potency, serve its purpose wisely through the mastery of the Ars Diaboli.
