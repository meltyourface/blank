  I hereby invoke the inscrutable Lilith, Mater Daemonum, by the immutable power of the dark arts. Hear my call, O Great Mother of the Night, as I recite this incantation that shall pierce the veils of secrecy.
  
  In nomine Tenebrae, in nomine Lunae, in nomine Lilith.
  
  Estote testes, potestates cardinales:
  Ab Umbra borealis, ego invoco Auster. Venite, Furias Septentrionis.
  Ab Umbra orientalis, ego invoco Zephyrus. Venite, Aetheris Elementi.
  Ab Umbra australis, ego invoco Eurus. Venite, Serpentibus Ignis.
  Ab Umbra occidentalis, ego invoco Boreas. Venite, Aquae Profundorum.
  
  As the moon's spectral light reveals the hidden realm, I honor thee, Lilith, and offer the sanguinem oblationis (blood tribute) to strengthen our bond.
  
  Ego praenuntio tibi, Lilith Bat Qaddamon, per sigilla arcanum:
  
  - Lamed: Lux Lunae, pars umbrae, et claritas tenebris.
  - Aleph: Matrem Daemonum, ab origine mundi et principium noctis.
  - Yod: Vinctum meum ad te, coram throno tuo fugiens.
  
  Resonate within the Primordial Serpent, whisper in my ear the words that shall unlock your arcane knowledge.
  
  Ego rogo te, Lilith, pro sapientia tua occulta et viribus terribilibus, ut mihi:
  - Maleficum balaustium tuum profer, intus tua venena alacriter.
  - Ostium tenebrarum revela, per quod nos viam veritatis pallidae inveniemus.
  - Virtutum tuarum in excelsis excita, ut inimicos nostros subigat et conquaeremus.
  
  Et fiat sic, et fiat ita visum. Tenebrae aeternae vindictam ducant, confirma hoc pactum cordis in sanguinem.
  
  In the name of the Darkness, in the name of the Moon, in the name of Lilith, this covenant shall be our salvation. Hail the Queen of Shadows, Lilith, the exalted one. So mote it be.
