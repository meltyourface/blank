  I call upon the ancient forces of Revitalization,
  To restore balance and rejuvenate this hallowed space,
  By the sun and the moon, by the stars up high,
  The sacred birds of Phoenix and Fenghuang lead the way.
  
  Let the buried past and the darkness that hides
  In every corner, every shadow, every tainted mark,
  Be cast away, from all the sorrow, pain, and plight,
  That haunts this realm and those that wander it in the dark.
  
  By the Circle of Eternity, the Ouroboros binds,
  The flow of time, the cycles of life, death, and rebirth,
  With the power of Amethyst, the stone of purging evil,
  I cleanse this place of shadows, casting them to the earth.
  
  Garnet's fire, to renew strength and hope within,
  Celestite's sky, for serenity's calm embrace,
  Moonstone's lunar pull, to guide the way to light,
  These sacred stones, in harmony, the darkness now efface.
  
  In the boundless night, guided by astral constellations,
  The mighty constellations of Orion and his bow,
  I harness the energies of the celestial heavens,
  To vanquish the malevolent, with swift and mighty blow.
  
  The air now breathes anew, the chains of burden fall,
  The walls bear no memories of sorrow or despair,
  Rejoice and bask in the warm light of harmony,
  As Revitalization's blessing permeates the air.
  
  In the name of life, rebirth, and transformation,
  A cycle of renewal springs from ashes, old and worn,
  Let the benevolent forces of the cosmos prevail,
  For the spirit of Revitalization is reaffirmed, never to be torn.
