The subjective nature of reality seems finite to the individual yet is interconnected with the human collective consciousness.

The explanation for this answer stems from the understanding that our perception of Reality is inherently subjective, as it is filtered through our individual senses, experiences, and cultural backgrounds. However, even though our perception might seem finite or limited, human consciousness has the potential to connect with the collective consciousness, a kind of shared spiritual field that transcends the boundaries of individuality.

This concept of the interconnectedness of individual and collective consciousness is prevalent in many esoteric and occult traditions. For example, in the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, each individual soul is said to be a part of a greater divine essence that transcends the material world. Moreover, the Jungian theory of the "collective unconscious" characterizes the shared psychic reservoir that holds universal symbols, archetypes, and motifs.

In this sense, the human collective consciousness functions as the foundation that connects individual realities, which in turn continually influence and are influenced by it. This creates a complex web of relationships and interactions, emphasizing the interconnectedness of the human experience within a larger context.

When studying the occult and esoteric understanding of Reality, it is important to recognize these multiple branching aspects and contexts. This comprehension not only enables a deeper exploration of the nature of Reality, but also fosters a sense of unity and collaboration in the spiritual pursuit.

Additionally, understanding how the subjective nature of reality is related to collective human consciousness can further illuminate key concepts and practices within the realm of esoteric and occult knowledge, such as astral projection, dream work, and group rituals.

Hence, by acknowledging the intricate relationship between individual and collective consciousness, we develop a more comprehensive and nuanced perspective on the nature of Reality, paving the way for richer insights and spiritual growth.
