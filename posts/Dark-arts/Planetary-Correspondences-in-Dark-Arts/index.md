| Planet       | Symbol                              | Color       | Material           | Occult Influence                            |
|--------------|-------------------------------------|-------------|--------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| Sun          | ⊙ or Circle with a dot in the center | Gold        | Gold, Amber        | Power, Illumination, Leadership, Creativity |
| Moon         | ☽ or Crescent Moon                  | Silver      | Silver, Pearl      | Intuition, Emotions, Transition, Mystery    |
| Mars         | ♂ or Circle with an arrow            | Red         | Iron, Red Jasper   | Courage, Assertion, Conflict, Passion       |
| Mercury      | ☿ or Crescents with a circle         | Violet      | Quicksilver, Opal  | Communication, Intellect, Travel, Commerce  |
| Jupiter      | ♃ or Cross with a Crescent           | Blue        | Tin, Lapis Lazuli  | Expansion, Growth, Wisdom, Luck             |
| Venus        | ♀ or Circle with a cross             | Green       | Copper, Emerald    | Love, Sensuality, Harmony, Fertility        |
| Saturn       | ♄ or Semi-circle/Cross combination  | Black       | Lead, Onyx         | Restriction, Discipline, Time, Transformation |

To enhance your Dark arts practice, you can use the colors, symbols, and materials associated with each planet to strengthen your rituals and spells. Integrate these correspondences into your altar and ceremonial workspaces to forge a deeper connection with the planetary energies and their occult influences.
