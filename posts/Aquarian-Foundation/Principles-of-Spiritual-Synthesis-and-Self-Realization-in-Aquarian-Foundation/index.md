- Principle of Spiritual Synthesis

The Principle of Spiritual Synthesis represents the core belief of the Aquarian Foundation that every individual must seek enlightenment through exploring the depths of their own consciousness and understanding the interconnectivity of all aspects of the universe.

The Aquarian Foundation is a spiritual movement that emphasizes the awakening and expansion of human consciousness by drawing spiritual knowledge and insight from various esoteric traditions and practices. The Principle of Spiritual Synthesis is central to this philosophy, as it encourages individual exploration and understanding of the self, and the broader context of the universe.

This principle is rooted in the ancient idea of the "perennial philosophy," which posits that there is a single, underlying truth that can be found in the teachings of all spiritual traditions. By synthesizing and integrating these teachings, seekers are able to tap into the innate wisdom of their own consciousness and perceive the interconnectedness of all things.

The Hermetic Theory of Correspondence, while related to the concept of interconnectedness, is more specifically focused on the idea that the universe is structured hierarchically and that principles found at one level of reality can also be applied to other levels. The saying "as above, so below" is a prime example of this theory.

The Declaration of Spiritual Independence emphasizes the importance of individual freedom and autonomy in the spiritual journey, asserting that each person has the inherent right to seek truth and enlightenment in their own way, without being hindered by any external authorities or dogmas.

The Sevenfold Path of Spiritual Unfoldment outlines a progressive series of spiritual practices and disciplines designed to help the individual develop their understanding of the self, the universe, and ultimately, attain spiritual enlightenment.
