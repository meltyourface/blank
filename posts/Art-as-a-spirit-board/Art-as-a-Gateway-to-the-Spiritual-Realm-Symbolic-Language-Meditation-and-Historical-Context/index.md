1. Symbolic Language in Art:

Art has long been an essential medium for humans to express thoughts and emotions that are not easily conveyed through verbal or written language. Symbolism in art plays a key role in connecting with spiritual entities, as it bridges the physical and spiritual planes. By employing symbols, artists create a visual language that transcends ordinary communication and resonates with both the conscious and subconscious minds of individuals engaging with the artwork.

The creative process itself can be a form of meditation, enabling artists to enter altered states of consciousness and tap into non-ordinary realms. In these states, it is believed that artists can receive messages, symbols, or impressions from spiritual entities, which are then translated into their artwork.

In this way, both the artist and the observer may participate in a dialogue with the spiritual realm, facilitated by the creative process and the viewer's engagement with the artwork.

Much of this artwork is designed to facilitate communication with spiritual beings or to depict esoteric knowledge.
